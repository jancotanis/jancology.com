The Police, 14 september, Amsterdam

September 15, 2007 | Comments (0)
The Police — Message in a Bottle; 14 September 2007; nokia 6230; © janco tanis

This was my first opportunity ever to go to a Police concert and it was worth it. Just three persons playing great sounding music — even in the ‘Arena’. I’ve searched the opening song, Message in a Bottle, on YouTube which was quite easy. I think its possible to see the whole concert there…
