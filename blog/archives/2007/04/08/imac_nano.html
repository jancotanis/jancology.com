iMac Nano!?

April 8, 2007 | Comments (0)

Apple has introduced a new small cheap computer for only € 299,— including a whole set of interfaces like:

  • HDMI (video and audio)
  • Component video
  • Optical audio
  • Analog RCA stereo audio
  • 10/100BASE-T Ethernet
  • USB 2.0
  • 802.11n wireless networking
  • Built-in IR receiver (works with included Apple Remote)

Disadvantage is that it carries a small disk (40GB) and that you need to hack it before the small box runs a full version of Mac OS X…
