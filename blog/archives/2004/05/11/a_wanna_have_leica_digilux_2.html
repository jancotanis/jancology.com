A wanna have: Leica Digilux 2

May 11, 2004 | Comments (0)

Phil Askey just posted his review of the Leica Digilux 2. This is a real nice camera with almost everythink I want to have:

  • Good lens (F2.0/2.4) with manual zoom and focus.
  • Big 2/3 5mpix ccd with not much noise!
  • Electronic viewfinder which is better wysiwyg then an slr camera.
  • BIG 2.5 inch LCD on the back
  • Smart flash design, which can also used to bounce the light on the ceiling
  • Looks good!
I said almost because it’s nearly twice as expensive as the Nikon D70 which has even less noise.
