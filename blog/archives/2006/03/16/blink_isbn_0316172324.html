Blink; ISBN 0316172324

March 16, 2006 | Comments (0)
Malcolm Gladwell

After reading “The Tipping Point” I got this book. It was a disappointment. The book reads through a series of stories where people have this so called “blink” moment without exactly explaining how you can train it.

These individual stories are quite entertaining but they miss somthing. The bigger picture which was definitely available in The Tipping Point is not there and makes this book much weaker!
