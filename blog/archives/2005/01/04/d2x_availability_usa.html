D2X availability (usa)

January 4, 2005 | Comments (0)

According to Nikon’s website the D2X will be available February 25th for $4,999 in the stores. This is a slight delay of what was expected.

However, … Nikon USA has now removed from their web site D2X price and release date information, the final price and delivery date of the camera has not yet been confirmed and the information was posted in error.
