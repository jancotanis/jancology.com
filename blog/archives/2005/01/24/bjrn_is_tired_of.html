Bjørn is tired of...

January 24, 2005 | Comments (0)
getting emails about the D2x…

“This announcement is meant to halt the current flood of e-mail requests and speculation regarding the new camera. I won’t answer any of them and it has become quite tiring to ward them off.

I can confirm — without breaking my NDA, that I have used production camera(s) — that I have tested nearly 20 lenses with this camera, and all of the information pertaining to the D2X camera as such will — eventually — be posted on my web site 25 February (coinciding with the sales launch in Europe). My personal D2X camera will be made available to me simultaneously and contrary to what people might think, I certainly have to pay up for the D2X like everyone else.” — Bjørn Rørslett

If you happen to drop by his site, you’ll definitely need to have a look at his large aperture photos in: “The need for Speed”. If you’re an “Equipment Measurbator” you need to look at the Nikon S3 with the ultrahigh-speed Rodenstock Heligon 50 mm f/0.75 lens.
