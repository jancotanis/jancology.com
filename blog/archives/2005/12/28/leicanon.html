LeiCanon

December 28, 2005 | Comments (0)

According the Japanese newspaper Asahi Shimbun, Canon will take over Leica Camera AG. In 2006 the merger will happen and in 2007 new products will hit the shelves. Will this solve the soft corner problem on Canon’s full frame DSLRs?

Update:Sorry for all the fuss, I got this information 3rd hand and can’t confirm this. From one point this would be good news as canon has some more money to spend then Leica at the moment. From the other side you would wonder what will be left from this authentic camera manufacturer when the merger is going to happen.
