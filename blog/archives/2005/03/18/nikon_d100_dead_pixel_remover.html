Nikon D100 dead pixel remover

March 18, 2005 | Comments (0)
For people who have found dead pixels on their Nikon D100 there is good news! There is a utility — Q50ImageAdjust — described at Mathias Pohl’s log that is able to remap the CCD. I think this might be very interesting as I had very good results with a Nikon CoolPix 5700 version of such a utility. You can download the windows RAR file.
