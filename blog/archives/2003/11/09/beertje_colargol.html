Beertje Colargol

November 9, 2003 | Comments (0)

I don’t know why but the tune of ‘Beertje Colargol’ keeps popping up in my head. This has happened to me already for years now. I even sing it for my daugther.

A few weeks back I went to Lebbis (Hans Sibbel) of the caberet duo Lebbis and Jansen. And even he used the song in this performance.

I just received a link to a good quality version of the song! Thanks Miranda :)

See Kinder TV for loads of other good stuff from your youth…

Oh, and not to forget; the lyrics: 

Ik ben beertje Colargol 
beertje dat kan zingen 
trala do re mi fa sol 
'k dans erbij het is te dol 

ik beleef zo veel 
lalara leer en speel 
met mijn fluitefluit 
blaas ik elk verhaaltje uit 
twiettwiet, twiettwiet, twiettwiet

nu gaan het beginnen 
trala do re mi fa sol
