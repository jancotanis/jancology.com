Number one on Google

July 22, 2003 | Comments (0)

I didn’t know it was that easy to get number one in the search ranks. Last week I added the scoop of the new Nikon D2H (which is released today). After adding the rumour to my site on saturday 12 july. I started to get hits from google. Using referer links I found out the location of the famous german sales folder which had a few images. After adding these, the hits bumped up and I started to get higher in the google ranks. Ok, I agree I won’t stay there. As a matter of fact I already lost some places (unless you search for Nikon D2H images).
